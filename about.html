Our History

The Steakhouse KL opened in 2019 serving high quality Argentinian-style charcoal-grilled steaks as well as a selection of traditional dishes cooked with country passion. Located in adjacent to Me'nate KL, The Steakhouse is one of the traditional steakhouses in KL exuding a warm, cosy and rustic ambience. It is a one stop venue for fine wines and perfectly prepared steaks.
